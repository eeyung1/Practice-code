## Personal AI Learning Ethics Commitment

### I will use AI to:
- Amplify my understanding after I've attempted problems independently
- Challenge my assumptions and explore alternative approaches
- Deepen my knowledge of concepts I've already grasped
- Work faster once I understand the fundamentals deeply

### I will NOT use AI to:
- Skip the struggle of learning and building mental models
- Copy code without thinking through the logic myself
- Avoid putting in effort to understand fundamentals
- Replace my own thinking and judgment
- Generate answers I haven't earned through my own work
